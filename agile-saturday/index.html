Agile Saturdays are seminar days that are held roughly once per quarter. What makes them different is that they are totally free for everybody and everyone can participate!

The events have 2-3 tracks of talks and activities by both local experienced agilists as well as several guests speakers from abroad.

The registration is usually on Eventbrite</a>.
