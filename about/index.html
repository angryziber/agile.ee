We are community of individuals, who believe in Agile Software Development, which is a much more efficient software development methodology. Via this organization we want to share experiences and unite like-minded people. For us this is the only natural way to think, work and sometimes to live.

We often meet at Agile Dinners and organize Agile Saturday conferences.
