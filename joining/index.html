Agile Estonia board has agreed to give one-year membership to all registered attendees of Agile Saturday events. Members are expected to participate in the discussions on mailing lists as well on Agile Dinner and other events.

Currently, we don't have membership fees, but members are expected to share their knowledge and help with some organizational aspects.
