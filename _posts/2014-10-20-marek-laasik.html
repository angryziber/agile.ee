Agile pitfalls in large organizations, how to not fail the programs</h2>

You will gain insight into more common pitfalls and learnings from running large architectural programs in agile environment and how to make them successful from company perspective.</p>

Session will give an insight to the practical side of running a large programs in agile environments.</p>

- How to manage the expectations of stakeholders, set achievable goals and deliver real products in predictable time-frame.

- Difficulties in defining the business value, making it clear and understandable for everyone.

- Role of a product owner in this environment and requirements for other roles.

- Avoiding duplication of work and making sure that the time is spent in the right place.</p>

The talk is based on the real life experience in running the programs that have involved around 100 developers and multiple scrum teams.</p>
